Benefits of VMA

VMA is a stand alone cloud based product that seamlessly captures all the patient health data and automatically integrates with the hospital's Electronic health records system. An adopter of VMA will immediately witness cost savings, user satisfaction, accuracy and timeliness of data.

Save time on data entry, see more patients

Based on our research physicians are spending additional 2-3 hours of uncompensated charting  time on a 4-hour block of direct patientcare. In other words, they are wasting 33-50% of their time on charting instead of direct patient care.  VMA can reduce this wasted physician time by half. Based on our model, every adopting physician can make additional    
$119, 000/ year by being able to provide additional 1 hour of direct patient care in a
half day clinic. 

Easy and Intutive to use

No more unfriendly screen interfaces and no "death of thousand clicks" ! VMA will
provide a user experience of the paper based templates that physicians seamlessly used prior to
the EHR systems while keeping all the invaluable benefits that the EHRs brought to patient
health records. This innovative approach of Virtual Medical Assistant empowers creating
progress notes and entering physician orders in both office and inpatient settings, rapidly and effortlessly.
